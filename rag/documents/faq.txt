FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS (FAQ)

Q: How do I reset my password?
A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your email, and follow the instructions sent to your inbox.

Q: Can I use the software offline?
A: Basic features work offline, but full functionality requires an internet connection for activation and updates.

Q: What payment methods do you accept?
A: We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and wire transfers for enterprise customers.

Q: How do I cancel my subscription?
A: Go to Account Settings > Billing > Cancel Subscription. You'll retain access until the end of your billing period.

Q: Is there a mobile app?
A: Yes! We have iOS and Android apps available in the App Store and Google Play Store.

Q: Can I transfer my license to another computer?
A: Yes, you can deactivate the license on one device and activate it on another. Maximum 3 active devices per license.

Q: Do you offer student discounts?
A: Yes, students with valid .edu email addresses get 50% off all plans. Contact support@company.com with proof of enrollment.

Q: What's your refund policy?
A: We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. See our refund policy document for full details.

Q: How do I contact support?
A: Email support@company.com, call 1-800-SUPPORT, or use live chat on our website. We're available 24/7.

Q: Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?
A: Yes, you can change your plan anytime from Account Settings > Billing. Changes take effect immediately.

For more questions, visit our knowledge base or contact support.
