# Ordervez Knowledge Base

This document describes Ordervez (https://ordervez.com), an all-in-one restaurant management system. Use it to answer questions about Ordervez products, capabilities, and how to get in touch.

## What is Ordervez?

**Ordervez** is an **All-in-One Restaurant Management System**. It is a unified platform that covers POS (point of sale), online ordering, delivery, and more. From online ordering and in-store operations to delivery monitoring and customer engagement, Ordervez streamlines every aspect of a restaurant so businesses have what they need to thrive.

---

## Product Overview

### Restaurant Management System for POS, Online Ordering, Delivery & More

Ordervez provides a **full-stack restaurant management system** that includes:

- **Online ordering** – Accept and manage orders placed online.
- **In-store operations** – Run your POS and day-to-day operations.
- **Delivery monitoring** – Track and manage delivery orders.
- **Customer engagement** – Tools to engage and retain customers.

The platform is designed as a **seamless integration ecosystem** that can be tailored to match your requirements.
