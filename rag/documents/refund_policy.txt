REFUND POLICY

Our company offers a comprehensive 30-day money-back guarantee on all products and services. 

Eligibility:
- All purchases made directly through our website are eligible for a full refund within 30 days of purchase.
- Refunds are processed to the original payment method within 5-7 business days.
- Digital products are eligible for refund if not downloaded or used.

Refund Process:
1. Contact our support team at support@company.com or call 1-800-REFUND
2. Provide your order number and reason for refund
3. Our team will review your request within 24 hours
4. Once approved, refund will be processed automatically

Exceptions:
- Custom orders or personalized products may not be eligible for refund
- Services already rendered cannot be refunded
- Refunds requested after 30 days will be considered on a case-by-case basis

For questions about refunds, please contact our customer service team.
